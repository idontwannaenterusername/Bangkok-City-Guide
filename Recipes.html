Step-by-step cooking instructions

🥢 Pad Kra Pao (Thai Basil Stir-Fry) — 💸 Budget-Friendly

Cost: 60–80 THB (≈ 2 USD) | Cooking time: 15 min

Ingredients

  • 200 g minced meat (chicken or pork)
  • 2 garlic cloves, chopped
  • 1 red chili
  • 1 tsp fish sauce
  • 1 tsp soy sauce
  • 1/2 tsp sugar
  • Thai basil leaves
  • 1 fried egg (optional)
  • Steamed white rice

Instructions

  1. Sauté garlic and chili in oil.
  2. Add minced meat and cook until browned.
  3. Stir in sauces and sugar.
  4. Add basil and cook until wilted.
  5. Serve over rice with fried egg.

🍛 Khao Pad (Thai Fried Rice) — 💰 Mid-Range

Cost: 100–150 THB (≈ 3–4 USD) | Cooking time: 20 min

Ingredients

  • 2 cups cooked rice (day-old)
  • 1 egg
  • 2 tbsp onion
  • 1 carrot, diced
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp oyster sauce
  • 100 g chicken, pork, or shrimp
  • Juice of 1/2 lime, cilantro

Instructions

  1. Heat oil and cook meat, set aside.
  2. Scramble egg, then add vegetables.
  3. Add rice and sauces.
  4. Return meat and mix well.
  5. Serve with lime and cilantro.

🍜 Tom Yum Goong (Spicy Shrimp Soup) — 💎 Homemade Comfort Food

Cost: 180–250 THB (≈ 5–7 USD) | Cooking time: 25 min

Ingredients

  • 200 g shrimp
  • 3 cups broth
  • 2 stalks lemongrass
  • 3 slices galangal or ginger
  • 3 kaffir lime leaves
  • 2 chilies
  • 2 tbsp fish sauce
  • 1 tbsp chili paste (Nam Prik Pao)
  • 1/2 cup coconut milk (optional)
  • Juice of 1 lime

Instructions

  1. Boil broth with lemongrass, galangal, lime leaves.
  2. Add chili paste and chilies.
  3. Add shrimp until pink.
  4. Stir in fish sauce and lime juice.
  5. Add coconut milk for creamy version.

🥥 Green Curry (Gaeng Keow Wan) — 🏮 Special Dish

Cost: 250–400 THB (≈ 7–11 USD) | Cooking time: 35 min

Ingredients

  • 400 ml coconut milk
  • 2 tbsp green curry paste
  • 200 g chicken or tofu
  • 1 cup eggplants or zucchini
  • 2 kaffir lime leaves
  • 1 tbsp fish sauce
  • 1 tsp sugar
  • Basil leaves and chilies

Instructions

  1. Heat half coconut milk, add curry paste.
  2. Add chicken and stir.
  3. Pour remaining coconut milk and vegetables.
  4. Simmer 15 min, season with fish sauce and sugar.
  5. Serve with jasmine rice.

Cooking Tips

  • Buy ingredients at local markets like Or Tor Kor or Chatuchak.
  • Pad Kra Pao and Khao Pad are ideal for small kitchens.
  • Use fresh herbs for authentic flavor.
  • Coconut milk quality affects Green Curry richness.
