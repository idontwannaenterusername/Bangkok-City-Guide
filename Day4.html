Main Experiences

Relaxation and shopping destinations

Traditional Thai Spa

Indulge in authentic Thai massage and spa treatments. Choose from luxury hotel spas or traditional massage schools like Wat Pho.

Duration: 2-3 hours | Cost: 800-3,000 THB | Book: Advance reservation recommended

Chatuchak Weekend Market

Massive weekend market with over 8,000 stalls selling everything from handmade crafts to vintage clothing and local art.

Open: Saturday-Sunday | Hours: 6:00 AM - 6:00 PM | Transport: BTS Mo Chit or MRT Chatuchak

MBK Center

Popular shopping mall known for affordable electronics, fashion, and souvenirs. Great for bargain hunting and air-conditioned comfort.

Hours: 10:00 AM - 10:00 PM | Floors: 8 levels | Transport: BTS National Stadium

ICONSIAM

Luxury waterfront shopping complex with high-end brands, riverside dining, and stunning views of the Chao Phraya River.

Hours: 10:00 AM - 10:00 PM | Features: Luxury brands, river views | Transport: Free shuttle boat

Shopping Guide

What to buy and where to find the best deals

Best Souvenirs:

  • Thai Silk: High-quality silk scarves, clothing, and accessories
  • Handicrafts: Wood carvings, silver jewelry, and traditional art
  • Spices & Tea: Thai curry pastes, exotic spices, and herbal teas
  • Elephant Pants: Comfortable and colorful traditional pants

Spa Treatment Options:

  • Traditional Thai Massage: 60-90 minutes of stretching and pressure points
  • Oil Massage: Relaxing full-body massage with aromatic oils
  • Herbal Steam: Detoxifying treatment with Thai herbs
  • Foot Massage: Perfect after days of walking and sightseeing

Shopping Tips:

  • Bargaining is expected at markets but not in malls
  • Check for authentic Thai silk certification
  • Compare prices at different stalls before buying
  • Bring cash for markets, cards accepted at malls
  • Check VAT refund eligibility for large purchases
  • Pack extra space in luggage for purchases
