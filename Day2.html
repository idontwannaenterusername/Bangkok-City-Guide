Main Attractions

Key experiences and what to expect

Damnoen Saduak Floating Market

Thailand's most famous floating market with vendors selling fresh fruits, local snacks, and souvenirs from traditional long-tail boats.

Distance: 100km from Bangkok | Best Time: 8:00-11:00 AM | Tour Cost: 1,500-2,500 THB

Amphawa Floating Market

More authentic and less touristy alternative, famous for fresh seafood and weekend evening atmosphere.

Distance: 80km from Bangkok | Open: Fri-Sun evenings | Specialty: Grilled seafood

Bangkok Khlong Tour

Explore Bangkok's canal network, see traditional stilt houses, and visit local communities along the waterways.

Duration: 2-3 hours | Cost: 500-800 THB | Departure: Various piers

Tips & Transportation

How to get around and make the most of your day

Getting to Floating Markets:

  • Organized Tour: Most convenient option, includes transport and guide (1,500-2,500 THB)
  • Public Transport: Bus to Damnoen Saduak (2-3 hours each way)
  • Private Car/Taxi: Expensive but flexible (3,000-4,000 THB round trip)

Khlong Tour Options:

  • Khlong San Saep: Public canal boat, authentic local experience
  • Thonburi Canals: Private tour boats from various piers
  • Wat Saphan Phut: Starting point for many canal tours

Important Tips:

  • Start early - floating markets are best in the morning
  • Bargain respectfully at markets
  • Bring cash in small denominations
  • Wear sun protection and comfortable clothes
  • Try local fruits and traditional snacks
