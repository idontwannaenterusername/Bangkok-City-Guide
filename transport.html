Transport & Getting Around

BTS/MRT for traffic-free travel, riverboats for sightseeing, and tuk-tuks for short trips.

BTS Skytrain & MRT

Fast & safe – use a Rabbit card or single-trip tokens. Peak hours get crowded.

Chao Phraya River Boats

Cheap and scenic – orange/green boats stop at major piers by the temples.

Tuk-tuks & Taxis

Negotiate tuk-tuk prices first; insist on the taxi meter for taxis.
