Main Attractions

Key sites and what to expect

Grand Palace

Thailand's most sacred temple complex and former royal residence. Marvel at intricate architecture and the revered Emerald Buddha.

Entry: 500 THB | Hours: 8:30 AM - 3:30 PM | Dress Code: Cover shoulders & legs

Wat Pho (Temple of the Reclining Buddha)

Home to the massive 46-meter golden Reclining Buddha and traditional Thai massage school.

Entry: 200 THB | Hours: 8:00 AM - 6:30 PM | Tip: Try authentic Thai massage

Chao Phraya River Cruise

Scenic boat ride offering views of illuminated temples and city skyline at sunset.

Duration: 1-2 hours | Cost: 300-800 THB | Best Time: 6:00-7:30 PM

Tips & Transportation

How to get around and make the most of your day

Getting There:

  • By BTS + Boat: BTS to Saphan Taksin, then Chao Phraya Express Boat to Tha Chang Pier
  • By Taxi: Direct to Grand Palace (expect traffic)
  • By Tuk-tuk: Fun but negotiate price first (150-200 THB)

Important Tips:

  • Dress modestly - no shorts, sleeveless tops, or revealing clothing
  • Bring water and wear comfortable walking shoes
  • Watch out for gem scams near tourist areas
  • Photography may be restricted in some temple areas
