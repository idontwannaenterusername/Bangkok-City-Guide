Pad Thai Recipe

The quintessential Thai noodle dish, balancing sweet, sour, and savory flavors.

Ingredients:
  • Rice noodles, shrimp or chicken, tofu, bean sprouts, chives
  • Tamarind paste, fish sauce, sugar
Recipe:
  1. Soak noodles until soft, then drain.
  2. Saut√© protein and tofu, then add noodles and sauce. Stir-fry until cooked.
  3. Add bean sprouts and chives. Serve with crushed peanuts and lime.

Tom Yum Goong Recipe

A classic hot and sour soup, bursting with fragrant herbs and spices.

Ingredients:
  • Shrimp, mushrooms, lemongrass, galangal, kaffir lime leaves
  • Chili paste, fish sauce, lime juice, cilantro
Recipe:
  1. Boil water with lemongrass, galangal, and lime leaves until fragrant.
  2. Add mushrooms and shrimp, cooking until the shrimp turns pink.
  3. Season with chili paste, fish sauce, and lime juice. Garnish with cilantro.

Mango Sticky Rice Recipe

A famous Thai dessert combining sweet sticky rice with fresh mango.

Ingredients:
  • Glutinous (sticky) rice, ripe mangoes
  • Coconut milk, sugar, salt
Recipe:
  1. Steam the glutinous rice until cooked.
  2. Heat coconut milk with sugar and salt, then mix with the cooked rice.
  3. Serve the sweet sticky rice with slices of fresh mango.

Som Tam Recipe

A spicy and refreshing green papaya salad that is a staple of Thai cuisine.

Ingredients:
  • Shredded green papaya, tomatoes, long beans, peanuts
  • Garlic, chilies, lime juice, fish sauce, palm sugar
Recipe:
  1. In a mortar, pound garlic and chilies.
  2. Add the remaining ingredients and gently bruise and mix everything together.
  3. Taste and adjust seasoning for the perfect balance of spicy, sour, sweet, and salty.
